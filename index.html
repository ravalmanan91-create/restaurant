About Our Restaurant

Discover the authentic taste of Kathiyawad with our traditional recipes passed down through generations

A Taste of Tradition

At Kathiyawadi Rasoi, we bring you the authentic flavors of the Kathiyawad region of Gujarat. Our recipes have been carefully preserved and passed down through generations, ensuring that every dish we serve is a true representation of this rich culinary heritage.

Founded in 2010, our restaurant has become a destination for food lovers seeking an authentic Kathiyawadi experience. We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to create dishes that are both flavorful and nutritious.

Whether you're a fan of spicy curries, sweet treats, or savory snacks, our extensive menu has something to satisfy every palate. Join us for a culinary journey through the heart of Kathiyawad.

Our Master Chefs

Meet the culinary experts who bring authentic Kathiyawadi flavors to your plate

Chef Rajesh Patel

Head Chef & Founder

With over 25 years of experience in Kathiyawadi cuisine, Chef Rajesh brings authentic recipes passed down through generations. His passion for traditional cooking methods and local ingredients has made our restaurant a destination for food lovers.

Chef Meena Joshi

Executive Chef

Specializing in traditional Kathiyawadi sweets and snacks, Chef Meena brings a feminine touch to our menu. Her expertise in balancing flavors and creating visually appealing dishes has earned her recognition in the culinary world.
